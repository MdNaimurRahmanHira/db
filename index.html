Database

IDM databases are time series databases and cover a broad range of reference market data from commodity and stock exchanges, currency rates, crypto-currencies, indices and more. To enable you to get only the data that you need, you can request selected contract columns, custom averages, date range and get historical values, metadata, spark line values, previous values etc. This allows you to build the reports that you need - easily and quickly.

We have access to proprietary data that is not listed on our public pages. Please let us know what data you need and from which data vendor. If you already subscriber via another platform we are happy to consider adding proprietary data to our service if it can be of benefit other subscribers.

Hundreds of thousands of market data time series are available from stock exchanges, commodity exchanges, banks, proprietary market data providers, independent pricing agencies as well as global currencies and indices and much more.
